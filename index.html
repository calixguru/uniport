Welcome to the Nigerian Society of Chemical Engineers
University of Port Harcourt

We’re excited to have you step into a community built on curiosity and problem solving. Here, you’ll be engage in hands on learning through our Technical Interest Group (TIG) and loads of innovative projects and collaborative initiatives that shape real world engineers. You’re very much welcome to take part in our workshops, seminars, debates/annual quiz and other student driven activities designed to strengthen your skills and broaden your perspective. Together, we can achieve a lot!

ABOUT NSChE UNIPORT

NSChE UNIPORT promotes academic excellence, research, industry exposure, and leadership development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the image and interests of the Chemical Engineering Department, strengthen affiliations with state, national, as well as professional bodies, encourage peaceful and productive relationships among students and staff, and provide a vibrant platform for knowledge exchange through seminars, conferences, meetings, and technical activities.

Our Vision

To build a unified, reputable, and well connected Chemical Engineering community that fosters excellence and collaboration among our students.
